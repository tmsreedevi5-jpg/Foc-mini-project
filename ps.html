Color

        Colour is the visual perception triggered
        in the brain when light, composed of different
        wavelengths, reflects off objects and enters
        the eye. It is not an inherent physical
        property of objects, but rather a sensation
        created by the mind processing light 
        reflected from surfaces, which absorb some
        wavelengths and reflect others.
